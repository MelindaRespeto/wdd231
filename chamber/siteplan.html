Site Plan: Gotha Paradise Resort

Site Name

Gotha Paradise Resort

The name reflects the luxurious, tranquil environment of Boracay Island, known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant atmosphere. The name conveys the idea of a dream-like getaway on one of the world’s most beautiful islands.

Optional Domain Availability: boracayparadiseresort.com (domain availability to be checked)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide potential guests with detailed information about the resort’s accommodations, services, activities, and amenities. It will allow visitors to book their stay easily and browse various special offers, room types, and resort experiences.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "What types of rooms are available, and how can I book a stay?"
  • Scenario 2: "What activities and experiences can I enjoy while staying at the resort?"
  • Scenario 3: "Are there any special promotions or discounts for guests?"

Additional Website Sections

Home Page: High-quality images, introduction to the resort.

Accommodations: Details of room types with descriptions and images.

Dining: Information about the resort's restaurants and menu options.

Activities & Experiences: Available activities such as water sports, spa services, and island tours.

Special Offers: Current deals and packages for guests.

Booking Page: A form for guests to book their rooms and services.

Contact Us: Contact form, location map, and resort contact information.

About Us: Mission, vision, and resort history.
